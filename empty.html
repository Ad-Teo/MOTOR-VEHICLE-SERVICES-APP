The application has got two interfaces, for the users(buyers and sellers) and then the admin. Each of the interfaces have various functions which have been implemented to meet the app's requirements.

Some of the different interfaces include: Home interface(enables users to view the uploaded products) sell interface(enables sellers to upload products) wallet(tracks users' bills and payments) profile(information about the user) purchase inerface( a buyer is able to request for loan or order with cash and decide on means of delivery)
