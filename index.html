INTRODUCTION

In our country, we realized that many people would love to sell their motor-vehicles, but they are unable to find market. Others would actually love to put them on loan. Bsides that, Many people would also love to own cars, but they donot have enough money to purchase them. And many car bonds cant trust them because of the different insecurities.

As a result, we decided to develop a mobile app to help different buyers be able to access different motor-vehicles on loan or cash depending on their situation. Sellers will be able to track different buyers since their respective credentials will be provided before being given loans. Sellers upload different vehicles and products for sale and awaits to approve different loans requested by buyers. Besides that, Buyers will also be able to make payments through the app and keep track of their bills.

DIFFERENT INRTERFACES OF THE APPLICATION

Here are some of the interfaces that are implemented for the different functionalities of the application

  • Profile interface(details of the user)
  • Wallet interface(billing transactions of the buyer)
    • Loan repayment interface
    • my wallet interface
  • Purchase interface(Requesting for a loan or buying on cash)
  • Home interface(displaying/viewing the different uploaded products)
  • Sell interface(uploading different products)

INSTRUCTIONS ON HOW TO USE THE APP

    Login into your account if you already have one. Otherwise register yourself inorder to access the app. An email will be sent for you to verify. If your a seller, go to the sell button and upload your products after which it appears on the homepage. For the case of a buyer, go to the home page, then view the different uploaded products. Choose the one suitable for you,click on the image and apply for a loan or to buy. After which you choose the mode of delivery and await for your loan/buy request to be approved.
